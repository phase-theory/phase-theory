Abstract

Phase Medicine is introduced as a new class of medical system in which healing is achieved not by repairing damage, counteracting disease, or stimulating recovery processes, but by restoring admissible biological phase continuity. Unlike classical medicine—which treats pathology after failure has occurred—Phase Medicine defines health as an admissible biological trajectory and illness as an inadmissible deviation. This paper formalizes Phase Medicine, establishes its dependence on Phase-Stabilized Synthetic Biology, Phase Memory, Phase Identity, Causality Filters, and Phase Governance, and demonstrates why chronic disease, irreversible injury, and degenerative decline are structurally impossible. In Phase-Native Engineering, patients are not repaired.

They are re-admitted to health.
