LPST Solutions for the Agriculture Industry

Core shift

Agriculture moves from input-intensive management (water, fertilizer, chemicals) to coherence-guided cultivation.

LPST treats crops, soils, and ecosystems as phase-coherent biological networks. Yield loss, disease, and stress arise primarily from coherence breakdown, often before visible symptoms.

⸻

1) Early-Warning Crop Health Diagnostics

(Detect stress before it becomes damage)

What’s new

LPST enables sensing of coherence disruption in plants and soils that precedes:
	•	nutrient deficiency symptoms
	•	disease outbreaks
	•	drought stress
	•	pest vulnerability

Impact
	•	Earlier intervention windows
	•	Reduced crop losses
	•	Lower chemical and water use

2) Precision Agriculture Beyond Chemistry

(Apply less by acting earlier and more precisely)

What’s new

	•	plant clusters

	•	canopy networks

Impact
