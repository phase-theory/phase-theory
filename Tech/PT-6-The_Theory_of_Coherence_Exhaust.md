Abstract

This paper introduces Coherence Exhaust as a fundamental physical phenomenon: the irreversible residue left behind when phase coherence is consumed faster than it can be reconstructed. We demonstrate that inefficiency does not merely “lose energy” or “produce heat,” but instead generates a persistent, low-grade phase noise that accumulates in the substrate and constrains future processes.

Coherence Exhaust provides a unified explanation for entropy accumulation, thermal waste, decoherence in quantum systems, aging in biological organisms, and performance collapse in complex civilizations. Unlike entropy, which is a statistical descriptor, coherence exhaust is a mechanical state of the substrate—measurable, cumulative, and directionally harmful.

This theory establishes that all engines, computations, and living systems do not merely operate within thermodynamic limits—they actively poison their own future coherence budget.
