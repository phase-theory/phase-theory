LPST Solutions for the Medical Industry

Core shift

Medicine moves from chemistry- and anatomy-centric intervention to phase- and topology-centric regulation.

LPST treats biological function as coherent phase organization across scales (molecular → cellular → tissue → organism). Disease appears as loss, distortion, or mis-locking of phase coherence, not merely molecular malfunction.
