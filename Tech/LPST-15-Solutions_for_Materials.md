LPST Solutions for the Materials Industry

Core shift

Materials are no longer defined primarily by composition, but by phase-topological organization.

LPST treats material properties as emergent from coherence, phase locking, and topology across scales—atomic → mesoscopic → bulk. Composition still matters, but structure-in-phase-space becomes the dominant design variable.

⸻

1) Topology-First Materials Design

(Design properties directly, not compositions indirectly)

What’s new

LPST allows materials to be classified and designed by:
	•	phase winding
	•	holonomy
	•	coherence robustness
	•	topological defect content

instead of trial-and-error chemistry.

Impact
	•	Faster discovery cycles
	•	Fewer failed formulations
	•	Direct targeting of desired properties (strength, conductivity, resilience)

⸻
