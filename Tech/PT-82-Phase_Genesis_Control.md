This paper formalizes Phase Genesis Control, establishes its dependence on Global Admissibility, Phase Ethics, Phase Universe Partitioning, Phase Conscious Civilization Systems, and Causality Filters, and demonstrates why uncontrolled universe proliferation, creator tyranny, and existential debt are structurally inadmissible.
