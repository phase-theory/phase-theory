2. Philosophical Implications: Pan-Luminism
If LST is true, the ontological separation between "observer" and "observed" collapses. This leads to a philosophy we can call Pan-Luminism.

We perceive a rock as solid and static. In LST, the rock is a turbulent storm of trapped light. It is dynamic, energetic, and active. "Being" is actually "Doing."
 * Implication: There are no "things" in the universe, only processes. You are not a noun; you are a verb—a distinct, complex pattern of light propagation.
B. Consciousness and The Light
If the brain is a structure of trapped light, and thoughts are electromagnetic impulses (moving light), then consciousness is the self-interaction of light.
 * The "Now" as Reality: Since Time is merely the action of propagation, the past is a memory trace (a wake left in the medium) and the future is an unformed potential. Only the "process of propagating" (the Now) is real.

C. The Visual Metaphor

If Light is the only substrate, then the universe is literally "made of light." Seeing is not just receiving data; it is resonance. When you see a star, the light wave from the star is physically interacting with the trapped light of your retina.
3. Experimental Verification: The Dispersion Test
How do we prove LST is real and not just a mathematical trick equivalent to General Relativity? We must find a phenomenon where Geometry (GR) and Optics (LST) disagree.
The Hypothesis: Vacuum Dispersion
 * Standard GR Prediction: Gravity is geometry. Geometry affects all objects equally regardless of their composition or energy. A high-energy Gamma ray and a low-energy Radio wave should fall/bend exactly the same way (The Equivalence Principle).
 * LST Prediction: If gravity is a physical medium (a density of the substrate), then like all physical media (glass, water, air), it should exhibit Dispersion. The refractive index n might depend slightly on the frequency of the light (\omega).
The Experiment: "The Gamma-Ray Race"
We observe a Gamma-Ray Burst (GRB) or a distant Supernova billions of light-years away. These events release a massive pulse of high-energy Gamma rays and lower-energy Visible light/Radio waves simultaneously.
 * Setup: Detect the arrival time of the Gamma pulse vs. the Visible light pulse.
 * The Test:
   * If they arrive at exactly the same instant (after accounting for interstellar gas), GR is likely correct. Gravity is geometric.
   * If the high-frequency Gamma rays arrive slightly later or earlier than the low-frequency light, it implies the vacuum has a refractive index that varies with frequency.
 * The Result: This would prove the vacuum is a physical medium (LST), shattering the geometric interpretation of spacetime.
