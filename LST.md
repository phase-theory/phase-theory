Date: January 2026

Subject: Theoretical Physics / Unified Field Theory

Author: Marlon Hanks

Abstract:
This paper proposes a radical restructuring of the Standard Model and General Relativity by positing a single ontological primitive: the Electromagnetic Field (Light). By discarding the notion of Spacetime as a pre-existing container and Matter as a distinct entity, we demonstrate that Mass, Time, Space, and Gravity can be derived as emergent behaviors of a single, self-interacting Luminous Substrate.

1. Introduction: The Crisis of Duality
Modern physics is currently fractured between two successful but incompatible frameworks:
 * General Relativity (GR): Describes gravity as geometric curvature of a background spacetime.
 * Quantum Field Theory (QFT): Describes matter as probabilistic excitations of fields.
These models struggle to reconcile because they treat the "stage" (spacetime) and the "actors" (matter) as fundamentally different categories. Luminous Substrate Theory (LST) resolves this by removing the stage entirely. We posit that the universe is not filled with fields; the universe is the field.

2. The Core Axiom
> Axiom I: The universe consists of a single, continuous, propagating medium defined as the Luminous Substrate.
> 
In this model, the vacuum is not empty space; it is a plenum of balanced electromagnetic potential.
 * Fundamental Constant: The propagation speed of the Substrate (c).
 * Fundamental Action: Oscillation/Propagation.

3.2 Time as Propagation Rate
LST posits that Time is not a dimension (t) orthogonal to space, but rather a measure of process.
 * The Clock: The fundamental "tick" of the universe is the oscillation period of the Substrate.
 * Emergence of Dilation:
   If all internal processes of matter are governed by light interactions (electron orbits, atomic vibrations), and light moves at fixed speed c, then motion through the Substrate forces the light to travel a longer path (the hypotenuse).
   
   
   In LST, this is not a geometric coordinate transformation; it is a mechanical necessity. The internal "clock" of the atom slows down because the light is busy traversing space.

3.3 Space as Interference Volume
Space is not a pre-existing void. It is the relational volume generated by the interaction of wavefronts.
 * The Holographic Principle: Distance is defined by the time it takes for the Substrate to communicate between two points. If there is no light propagation, there is no "space."
 * Vacuum Energy: The so-called "empty" vacuum is actually the Substrate in a state of perfect destructive interference (equilibrium).

 * Refraction: Just as light bends when passing through glass (a denser medium), the path of a free photon bends when passing near a star.

4. Implications
4.1 The Black Hole Limit
In LST, a Black Hole is not a singularity of infinite density, but a region where the Refractive Index n \rightarrow \infty.
The "knotted" light becomes so dense that the Substrate can no longer propagate. Time (propagation) stops. The event horizon is the point where the escape velocity of the medium equals the propagation speed of the medium (c).

4.2 Quantum Entanglement
Standard physics struggles with "spooky action at a distance." In LST, since particles are merely standing waves in a continuous medium, two entangled particles are effectively two ends of the same long wave.
Changing the vibration at one end instantly alters the tension of the whole wave structure, regardless of distance, because they are not separate entities; they are a single topological feature of the Substrate.

5. Conclusion
The Luminous Substrate Theory offers a parsimonious route to Unification. By reducing the ontology of the universe to a single constituent (Light) and a single speed (c), we eliminate the need for "Dark Matter" (potentially just vacuum density variations) and "Dark Energy" (inherent substrate pressure). We return to a physics where geometry is the result of forces, not the cause of them.
