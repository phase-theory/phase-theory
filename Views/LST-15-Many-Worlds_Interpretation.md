In Luminous Substrate Theory (LST), the traditional "Multiverse" (Specifically the Everett "Many-Worlds Interpretation" where every decision spawns a new timeline) does not exist.
Why?
 * Determinism: As established in Paper F (Quantum Knot Theory), particles are not probabilistic clouds; they are deterministic knots. There is no "wave function collapse," so the universe never splits.
 * Unity: As established in Paper E (Topological Unity), the universe is a single, continuous, interconnected solid block of Light. There is only one "Substrate."
However, LST replaces the "Multiverse" with a concept that is structurally different but functionally similar: "Spectral Laminations."
We are not living in one of many separate bubbles. We are living on one of many overlapping channels.
The LST Alternative: The Laminar Universe
Instead of "Parallel Universes" (Spatial separation), LST posits "Orthogonal Frequencies" (Spectral separation).
The Analogy: Fiber Optic Multiplexing
Imagine a single fiber optic cable. You can send a Red Laser signal and a Blue Laser signal down the same cable simultaneously.
 * The Red Signal (Us): Contains our stars, our planets, our bodies.
 * The Blue Signal (Them): Contains a completely different set of stars and planets.
 * The Interaction: Because the frequencies are different (Orthogonal), the Red light passes right through the Blue light without scattering. To the Red people, the Blue universe is invisible (ghostly).
1. The Physics: Orthogonal Phase States
The Substrate is a high-bandwidth medium. It can support multiple independent "Realities" occupying the exact same volume of space, provided they are Phase-Shifted by 90 degrees or separated by a harmonic interval.
 * Layer A (The Alpha Lamination): Our reality. Base Frequency f_0.
 * Layer B (The Beta Lamination): A superimposed reality. Base Frequency f_1.
 * The Interface: We occupy the same space. A person in Layer B could be walking through your living room right now. You don't see them because your electrons (knots of light) are vibrating at f_0, and theirs are vibrating at f_1. The waves pass through each other.
2. Evidence: The "Ghost" Mass
We previously identified Dark Matter as "High-Complexity Knotted Light" (Paper G).
However, some Dark Matter might actually be "Leakage" from Layer B.
 * Gravitational Cross-Talk: While electromagnetic waves (light/matter) usually cannot interact across layers, Gravity (Refraction of the Substrate) affects the entire medium.
 * The Effect: A massive star in Layer B bends the Substrate. In Layer A (our world), we see "Gravity without a Star." We call this Dark Matter. It is the shadow of a neighbor we cannot see.
3. Engineering Implication: The Phase-Shift Drive
Standard Sci-Fi talks about "Portal Guns" to jump between universes. LST suggests "Tuners."
The Device: The Chromatic Shifter.
 * Mechanism: If we can alter the vibrational frequency of the electrons in a ship (using a System-Wide Resonant Field), we can "detune" ourselves from Layer A and "tune in" to Layer B.

   * Travel: Layer B might be empty space. You shift to B, fly through the "solid" Earth of Layer A (which is now ghost-like to you), and shift back.
