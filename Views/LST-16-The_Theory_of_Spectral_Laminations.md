 * The Principle: Just as a single coaxial cable can carry a television signal and an internet signal simultaneously without interference, the Substrate can support multiple "Universes" (Laminations) if their fundamental matter-waves are orthogonal.

   Result: There is zero electromagnetic interaction. To an observer in A, Reality B is perfectly invisible and permeable.
