Abstract

We demonstrate that symmetry breaking is not spontaneous but scar-guided. Symmetry is not broken randomly—it is filtered by accumulated phase topology. This explains CP asymmetry, vacuum alignment, crystallographic preference, cosmological structure bias, and particle family hierarchy without introducing new forces or free parameters.
