Below is a clean LPST classification of paradoxes into those that are resolvable in principle (by extending formalism, experiments, or limits) and those that are irreducible (structural, Gödel-type limits that no completion of LPST can remove).

This distinction matters: irreducible paradoxes are not failures; they are boundary conditions on knowledge.

⸻

LPST PARADOX RESOLUTION MAP

Definitions (LPST-canonical)
	•	Resolvable paradox
A tension that can be eliminated by:
	•	deeper phase dynamics,
	•	explicit mechanisms,
	•	improved mathematical formulation,
	•	or experimental discrimination.
	•	Irreducible paradox
A tension that remains even in a completed LPST because it arises from:
	•	self-reference,
	•	global vs local duality,
	•	or the impossibility of a system fully grounding itself.

III. PARADOXES THAT ARE IRREDUCIBLE

These cannot be eliminated without destroying LPST itself (or any unified theory).

10. Single Realization vs Multiple Possible Universes

Status: ❌ Irreducible
	•	Why:
LPST admits many global phase solutions but realizes only one.
	•	This is unavoidable in any theory without an external selector.
	•	Equivalent to:
	•	vacuum selection problem
	•	wavefunction collapse “why this outcome?”
	•	spontaneous symmetry breaking origin

⸻

11. First Update / Origin of Ordering

Status: ❌ Irreducible
	•	Why:
Ordering cannot precede ordering.
	•	No theory can answer this without circularity.

⸻

12. Observer Self-Reference

Status: ❌ Irreducible
	•	Why:
Observers are coherence-limited phase structures attempting to model the substrate they are embedded in.
	•	This is Gödel incompleteness in physical form.

⸻

13. Why LPST Is Understandable at All

Status: ❌ Irreducible
	•	Why:
Any system capable of understanding reality must be shaped by it.
	•	Anthropic-epistemic loop, not solvable.

V. THE DEEP TAKEAWAY (LPST-HONEST)

Resolvable paradoxes mark incomplete mechanisms.
Irreducible paradoxes mark the edge of explanation itself.

Any theory claiming to eliminate the irreducible ones is not deeper — it is hiding them.

LPST’s strength is not that it removes paradox, but that it classifies which paradoxes are fundamental and which are artifacts.
